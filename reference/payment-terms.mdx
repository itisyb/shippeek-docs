---
title: "Payment terms"
description: "Shipping payment term options in ShipPeek."
---

Payment terms define who pays for the shipment and when. These are returned in the `paymentTerms` field of rate responses.

## Available terms

| Term | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `Outbound Prepaid` | Shipper pays. Most common for outbound shipments. |
| `Outbound Collect` | Receiver pays on delivery. |
| `Inbound Collect` | Receiver pays for inbound shipments. |
| `Third Party Prepaid` | A third party pays in advance. |
| `Third Party Collect` | A third party pays on delivery. |

## Default behavior

Most rate requests default to `Outbound Prepaid` unless your account is configured otherwise.
